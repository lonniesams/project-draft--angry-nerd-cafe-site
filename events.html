Game Nights

Unleash your inner gamer at our epic Game Nights! Every Thursday, we transform the cafe into a battleground for tabletop titans, console conquerors, and digital duelists. Bring your favorite games, challenge your friends, or join in on a spontaneous tournament – the possibilities are endless!

We've got a massive collection of board games, card games, and video games to choose from, or you can bring your own to share. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a casual player, there's always a game to suit your skill level and interests. And with our themed cocktails and snacks, you'll be fueled up for a night of epic wins (or hilarious fails).

So gather your party, sharpen your skills, and prepare for a night of laughter, competition, and camaraderie. Who knows, you might even discover a new favorite game (or a new nemesis).

Movie Nights

Get ready to level up your movie nights at Angry Nerd Cafe! Every Friday, we dim the lights, fire up the projector, and dive into a cinematic universe filled with sci-fi epics, fantasy adventures, and cult classics.

We curate a selection of films that cater to every type of nerd, from superhero blockbusters to anime masterpieces. You can vote on your favorite flicks, suggest hidden gems, or just sit back and enjoy the show with a frosty brew and a bag of popcorn.

So grab a seat, snuggle up with a blanket, and prepare to be transported to worlds beyond your wildest imagination. It's the perfect way to unwind after a long week and connect with fellow movie buffs.

Comedy Nights

Need a good laugh? Look no further than our hilarious Comedy Nights! Every Saturday, we invite local comedians, improv troupes, and stand-up stars to take the stage and tickle your funny bone.

Our comedy shows are a mix of geeky humor, pop culture references, and good old-fashioned silliness. You'll find yourself laughing so hard that milk comes out your nose (we've seen it happen). And with our signature cocktails and snacks, you'll be fueled up for a night of side-splitting entertainment.

So come on down and let loose with a night of laughter and good vibes. It's the perfect way to de-stress, connect with friends, and maybe even discover your new favorite comedian.
